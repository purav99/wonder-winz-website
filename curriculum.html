Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)

The EYFS framework sets the standards for the learning, development and care of children from birth to 5 years old in the UK – and we bring this excellence to Dubai.

Personal, Social & Emotional Development

Helping children develop a positive sense of themselves and others.

Communication and Language

Fostering listening, understanding, and speaking skills in a language-rich environment.

Physical Development

Developing coordination, control, movement, and healthy habits.

Literacy and Mathematics

Exploring reading, writing, number sense, and patterns through play.

Understanding the World

Encouraging exploration, observation, and discussion about the world.

Expressive Arts and Design

Creating through music, movement, art, role play, and storytelling.

Teaching Approach

  • Play-based and child-initiated learning
  • Individualized Learning Plans
  • Observational Assessments
  • Focus on school readiness and holistic development
